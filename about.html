About Us

FurniFree was created by Alex Oh, Raneesh Adulla, Rehan Pagarkar, Aarush Shah, and Vaibhav Herugu. We created this website so that people that didn't have beds, matresses, or sofas could gain access to simple things like these. We wanted to help the world, and kindess was the best way to do that. A small act of kindness can be as simple as putting a coat around a young boy's shoulders, and our way of giving back is to give furniture.
